Hello and welcome to Shinawatra International University (SIU)

My name is Arthur Kwizera a 3rd year student from Uganda offering Bachelor of Science in Management Technology with an Architectural technology major. A second thought will perhaps strike in your mind “why SIU?” Firstly, I would consider myself a keen advocate of SIU. Studying Architecture in SIU has contributed towards my personal development in ways I could hardly imagine when I first left Uganda! At SIU, I have gained a rewarding experience providing me with a deep and thorough fundamental academic knowledge, something which I continue to practically enjoy here in my daily life. “Picking a right University can be overwhelming because you’re essentially making a decision of where you will be spending the next four to five years of your life, but Shinawatra International University has definitely been the right choice for me. SIU has a great learning environment fostering both the students’ personal and professional excellence. The University offers tremendous opportunities for students to succeed in their path, from fellowships to conferences and unique collaborations. Through the combination of excellent academic staff, well structured courses and a friendly and active student community – SIU has been a wonderful experience for which I am very thankful. The decision to study Architecture at SIU is one that I definitely don’t regret. Looking around the campus, Food is superb, the facility is beautiful (both academic and recreational; talk of state of the art modern equipped classrooms, dormitories, gym, football pitch, basketball and tennis courts to mention but a few) and the teaching is top-notch. The flexibility of the University’s curriculum leaves absolutely nothing to be desired – SIU is one university where students have an opportunity to adjust their initially registered courses to any alternative course of their choice upon request, Something I personally benefited from because initially, I enrolled as a business student but later switched to architecture. At SIU, I’ve been fortunate enough to meet, study and interact with multi-national students. This experience has provided me with an excellent environment to put the content of my Architectural concept to practical use through design since each student has their own perspective towards design therefore a great forum to learn. For the two years I’ve been at SIU, I’ve found there to be a great rapport between students and staff, who are always encouraging and supportive of new ideas and academic endeavors. I would largely attribute this to a well internationally blended atmosphere at SIU. The University is very impressed with the sheer wealth of knowledge and talent students brings with them and interestingly, each one’s ability is enrooted on spot; usually through a series of SIU’s specially designed activities such as the SIU talent hunt which is aimed at developing and appreciating students’ individual ability mainly through music, dance, poetry acting and drama prize competitions. This has proved fundamental to the success of SIU consequently becoming a brand name as far as University education in Thailand is concerned, so far an un rivaled routine destination for International Students in Thailand. With SIU’s interactive small size class structures I have been able to learn from my fellow colleagues and enjoy the lecture’s attention in detail, this has been essential to the development of my invaluable organizational, teamwork and leadership skills. Lecturers also introduce students to the real world outside the classroom; For instance I’ve had an opportunity to attend numerous academic seminars and workshops all courtesy of SIU. I cannot overemphasize the value of discussing your knowledge with those outside your university. It forces you to think outside of the box, and to communicate your ideas with universal clarity. Most importantly, I would suggest that the Shinawatra International University brand is well recognized among the International Universities. This’ traced from a series of partnerships SIU has executed with other powerful International Universities such as the European University In Geneva and SIMAD University In Somalia. Finally, as the world in which we live continues to evolve, one’s ability to adapt and to demonstrate strengths that differentiate oneself has increasingly become synonymous with one’s original roots. Subsequently, the design world has become increasingly important. All the great brains behind signature master pieces of modern architecture e.g. The Palm Islands, Sydney opera house and the Guggenheim museum are a true reflection of what a great university is capable of producing and SIU is no exception. Architecture course at SIU has been the beginning of my life-long commitment to learning and there is no doubt in my mind that my time at SIU will be of continuous value as I continue to pursue my personal ambitions and career goals.
