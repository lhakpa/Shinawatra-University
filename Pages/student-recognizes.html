President Recognizes Triumph Achieved by Student Representatives

At the “42nd Thailand University Games, Nontri Games” during January 15 – 24, 2015 at Kasetsart University Kamphaeng Saen Campus, SIU student representatives participated in 2 sport categories including crossword and chess. As the final result, the participated students from Shinawatra University prosperously won 3 bronze medals in male international chess, female Thai chess and female checker. Praises to this outstanding performance owned by this group of student representatives. Hence, President Prof. Dr. Voradej Chandarasorn appreciates the student representatives’ dedication by presented cash reward and certificate of achievement to individuals accordingly.
