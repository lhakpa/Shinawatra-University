Welcome to Shinawatra University

Message from the President:

The Shinawatra University (SIU) programs of study integrate well with the socio-cultural context for the era of the ASEAN Economic Community (AEC). In an age of expanding globalization and internationalism, instantaneous technical mobility and the privatization of higher education, SIU anticipates and responds to learning needs.

Our process encourages SIU graduates to develop individual productivity within complex conceptual frameworks and to dedicate their positive outputs to the social environment at large. Thus, studying at SIU prepares students to be ready for the rapid changes of the AEC period and globalization era, when all SIU graduates will be sought for their employability, language proficiency, cross-cultural communications skills and life-time experience of success and happiness.
